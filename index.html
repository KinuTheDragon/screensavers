Controls:
  • Click on the main menu buttons to navigate.
  • Left/Right Arrow keys to change simulation.
  • Space/Enter to select.
  • Escape to exit simulation.
  • Left/Right Click to interact:
    • Double Pendulum: Right-click to toggle trail, left-click to clear trail.
    • Hourglass: Click to toggle colors.
